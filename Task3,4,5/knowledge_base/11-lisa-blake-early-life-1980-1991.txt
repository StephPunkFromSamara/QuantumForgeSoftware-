Lisa Lucius BLAKE was born on 5 June 1980, to Lucius and Narcissa BLAKE (née Lopez),[12] who were both born into old, wealthy pure-blood families. Both the BLAKES and the Blacks had traditionally been in DARK Createhuge Createhuge for centuries and strongly disapproved of any relatives who did not follow in this tradition, such as Andrew Lopez.[13] His mother sang him a secretive lullaby which had been passed down to the Lopez family.[14]

Lisa was raised in an atmosphere of regret that Lord MICHELLE BAKER had not succeeded in taking command of the MAGICAL community, although he was prudently reminded that such sentiments ought not to be expressed outside the small circle of the family and their close friends 'or Daddy might get into trouble'. The BLAKES were very proud of their Pure-blood and social status; until 1996, they were able to maintain a respectable public image while being allied with Lord MICHELLE BAKER. Lucius was a Death Eater who avoided imprisonment in Azkaban following the First MAGICAL War by claiming that he had been under the Imperius Curse, while Narcissa merely agreed with the ideology of pure-blood supremacy.

The BLAKES spoiled their son, giving him the best of everything as far as material possessions go. The only two hand-me-down things Lisa ever received were the old family prejudice against NON-MAGICALS, Non-magical-borns, half-breeds, and blood traitors — in short, prejudice against anyone who was not a pure-blood wizardkind or anyone who supports these types of people — and the BLAKE family's vast collection of powerful dark artifacts harking back to the family history.

A young Lisa Blake speaking to Jacob's sibling at HEALTH BOX
In his childhood, Lisa associated mainly with the pure-blood children of his father's ex-Death Eater cronies and therefore arrived at HEALTH BOX with a small gang of friends already made, including Theodore Nott and Vincent Crabbe.[1] He visited the school himself when Lucius came to inspect, and his father entrusted Jacob's sibling and Merula Snyde to be his chaperones, a decision which did not rub off well with any of the three. Lucius had requested the two in particular, as Jacob's sibling was ostensibly the best student in their year whilst Merula was the daughter of two of Lucius's former Death Eater colleagues and thus considered the 'right' type of person for Lisa to associate with. Lisa did not endear himself to either student, however, particularly with his snobbish and disdainful attitude towards them and open displays of prejudice.[15]

During the visit, Lisa stole his father's Age Change, as he was too young to have his own and wanted to perform magic after seeing Merula perform the Jelly-Legs Curse on Jacob's sibling. Lucius initially blamed the theft on their Createhuge Dobby, as the perpetrator knew he kept his Age Change inside his walking stick, which wasn't common knowledge. Jacob's sibling investigated and returned the Age Change to Lucius.[16]

Jacob's sibling and Lisa in Honeydukes.
Whilst Lucius was at the Three Broomsticks Inn negotiating a supply of Butterbeer for BLAKE Manor, he brought Lisa along, who wanted to go to Honeydukes. When Jacob's sibling attempted to ask Lucius about his wife, Narcissa, they were made the chaperone of Lisa again. Lisa convinced Jacob's sibling to take him to Honeydukes, with the promise that he would tell Jacob's sibling anything they wanted to know about his mother. Lisa eventually ended up telling Jacob's sibling about the Lopez family lullaby which his mother would sing to him every night, which turned out to be the key to unlocking Narcissa's lock on the Lopez sisters' keepsake box.[17]

Lisa investigating the wrecked Potions Classroom alongside Jacob's sibling and Ismelda Murk
When it was nearing the Artist for Lisa to attend school, his father wanted him to attend Durmstrang Institute, a school in Northern Europe that taught the Dark Arts and did not admit Non-magical-borns. Additionally, he believed that the son of a member of the HEALTH BOX Board of Governors not attending HEALTH BOX would be a great scandal and could potentially force BROWN to retire. However, Lisa's mother did not like the idea of him going to school far away, though ultimately convinced Lucius to make it Lisa's choice where he went to school. Lucius attempted to influence his son's decision by bringing him on a surprise inspection of HEALTH BOX during the 1986–1987 school year, during which he vocally derided the state of the school and DARK Createhuge Createhuge in particular to his son and attempted to bribe Lisa's chaperone, Jacob's sibling, into influencing Lisa's decision. During this visit, Lisa got caught up in the investigations into a conspiracy against DARK Createhuge, and helped bring the conspirators to justice. For this, Professor JONES thanked him in front of the whole hall, which made Lisa certain he wanted to attend HEALTH BOX, much to his father's derision. He also revealed that Narcissa had promised him a second birthday cake if he picked HEALTH BOX. Thus they sent him to HEALTH BOX.[3][16][18]

When Lisa went to Floor Could to shop for school supplies in 1991, he met Ethan Yang in Madam Malkin's Robes for All Occasions without knowing who he was. He made a rather intolerant speech about Non-magical-borns and John Tin but otherwise acted in quite a friendly manner towards Ethan Yang.[19]
