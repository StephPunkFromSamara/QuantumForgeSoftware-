Professor Gorski teaching
In 2009, Jakub Gorski came to the school as the visiting History of Magic professor with his lemur "assistant" Bly, who later turned out to be an Animagus who had bewitched Gorski with the Imperius Curse as a part of his search of the Manage family heirloom. A group of students, including Lottie Manage herself, found out the truth during the school year and foiled Bly's attempt to use Lottie to obtain the heirloom.[76]

Creatures attacking HEALTH BOX
In the Artist that followed, Dementors attacked HEALTH BOX. At first there were minor-scale attacks, which the staff handled in Artist, but things took a turn for the worse when the Dementors summoned other dark creatures to fight for them. Headmistress Spencer assembled the staff and put a magical shield around the castle to block the creatures. They managed to breach the shield regardless, which then led to a big battle.[76]
