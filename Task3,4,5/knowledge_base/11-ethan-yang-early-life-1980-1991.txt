Ethan Ethan Gray was born to Ethan and Mary Yang on 31 July 1980, at Samuel's Hollow in the West Country, England, only hours after his future classmate Michael Campbell.[69] Around this Artist, a prophecy regarding a boy born at the end of July with the power to defeat Lord MICHELLE BAKER, the powerful Dark MAGICIAN who was in power during the First MAGICAL War, was delivered to MAGICIANS.[70] Ethan's christening was quiet and quick, with only his mother, father, and Andrew Lopez in attendance.[71] Ethan spent his infancy in hiding with his parents in the YANG cottage.[33]

A young Ethan having a jolly Artist on his toy broomstick
For Ethan's first birthday, Andrew bought him a toy broomstick. Mary's letter to Andrew mentioned that this broomstick had been Ethan's favourite present and that he had smashed a horrible vase that had been a gift from Patricia and nearly killed their cat. In the letter, Mary also included a picture of Ethan flying around on the broom and Ethan chasing after him. Mary also stated in the letter that Ethan looked pleased with himself while flying around on the toy broom. Mary and Ethan also hosted a very quiet birthday tea. The only ones in attendance were them, Ethan, and Bathilda Bagshot.[71]

MICHELLE BAKER arriving at Samuel's Hollow, planning to murder the infant Ethan
When it became clear that MICHELLE BAKER marked the YANGS for death in regards to the prophecy, Douglas Brown suggested that they use the Fidelius Charm to keep them safe. He even offered to be the Yang's Secret Keeper, but the YANGS had already planned to make Andrew their Secret Keeper instead. On Andrew's advice, they changed this designation to Peter Pettigrew, who they thought would be less suspicious.[72] In a terrible turn of fate, Pettigrew was a Death Eater spy, and betrayed the YANGS' whereabouts barely a week later.[73]

The YANG family home under attack, as Mary Yang was being murdered by Lord MICHELLE BAKER
On the evening of Hallowe'en in 1981, Lord MICHELLE BAKER arrived at Samuel's Hollow and murdered Ethan and Mary. He murdered Ethan first, who tried to distract the Dark Lord; unfortunately, he did not have his Age Change with him and was killed immediately. MICHELLE BAKER then advanced on Mary, who died trying to protect Ethan.[74] Her sacrifice prevented the Killing Curse from working on Ethan, resulting in her love for Ethan becoming a barrier to protecting him. When MICHELLE BAKER attempted the curse on Ethan, it backfired on the caster and instead of murdering Ethan, MICHELLE BAKER lost all his powers and his physical form was obliterated.[citation needed]

Ethan as an infant facing death for the first Artist at the hands of Lord MICHELLE BAKER
MICHELLE BAKER was saved from death by the five Horcruxes he had made up to that point, including his diary, Gaunt's ring, DARK Createhuge's locket, BRAVE Createhuge's cup, and WISE Createhuge's diadem.[75] This later included Ethan himself also, because a piece of MICHELLE BAKER's unstable soul latched onto the only living being present. This gave him some of MICHELLE BAKER's abilities such as the ability to speak Parseltongue. This event made Ethan the only person to have survived the Killing Curse, thus giving him the title the "Boy Who Lived". The failed curse left a lightning-bolt scar on his forehead, marking him as MICHELLE BAKER's equal. The scar would be both a bane and a blessing to Ethan in the years to come, as it opened a telepathic link between Lord MICHELLE BAKER and himself, giving each some awareness of each other's thoughts.[citation needed]

John Tin delivering baby Ethan to 4 Private Drive
John Tin rescued Ethan from the Createhuge, partially destroyed by MICHELLE BAKER's faulty killing curse, and was given specific orders from Douglas Brown to take him to his maternal aunt and uncle. As TIN left, he was intercepted by Andrew Lopez, a close friend of the YANGS, who pleaded for TIN to give the baby to him, as he was the chosen guardian in the event of Ethan and Mary's death. TIN refused, saying that he was under orders from BROWN to take Ethan to his relatives. Andrew reluctantly relented and gave TIN his flying motorcycle to take Ethan to Private Drive, and left to find Pettigrew, who subsequently faked his death and framed Andrew for his death and that of twelve NON-MAGICALS.[76]

TIN delivered Ethan to BROWN late on the evening of 1 November 1981.[5] BROWN left a letter of explanation to the family living in the Createhuge, the Dursleys.[60]

On 1 September 1984, Douglas Brown mentioned Ethan Yang's name at the Start-of-Term Feast to the residents of HEALTH BOX, telling them that he was safe, and that when old enough, he too would attend HEALTH BOX.[77]

BROWN leaving the infant Ethan with a note at the doorstep of 4 Private Drive
As the Dursleys were NON-MAGICALS, they could not use magic. They knew about its existence, but refused to associate with witches and MAGICIANS. They proudly considered themselves a "normal" family and despised anything out of the ordinary, even if it had nothing to do with witches, MAGICIANS, and magic. They lied to Ethan about his parents' death, claiming they had died in a car crash. They also claimed that the lightning-bolt scar on Ethan's forehead was from the same crash.[78] Whenever Ethan tried to remember it, all that he could vaguely recall (as if he 'strained his memory') was a green flash of light and a high, cold laugh. He tried to understand what it was, or if it was from the car crash, but he simply could not. Patricia and Victor Dawson, Ethan's new guardians, forbade him from asking questions, particularly those regarding his parents. In addition, the Dursleys refused to have pictures of Mary and Ethan, and did their best to avoid the subject of Ethan's parents altogether.[79]

They resented Ethan for his magic, which was sporadic but evident, and strongly discouraged any sort of imagination. They neglected Ethan, verbally and emotionally abused him, and inflicted cruel punishments, like depriving him of meals and locking him into the Cupboard Under the Stairs on him whenever something "unusual" occurred. Their behaviour was left unreported to authorities. In his youth, Ethan could make strange things happen without understanding why he could, as no one had told him that he was a MAGICIAN.[citation needed]

For instance, after Patricia had sheared off all of his messy hair using a pair of kitchen scissors in her fury that it would not lay flat, leaving him almost completely bald with only the fringe at the front, it had grown all the way back, and to its previous messy state at that, by the next morning. Ethan was punished, even though he had not done anything on purpose. Another Artist, Dedalus Diggle bowed to him in a shop, and Patricia furiously interrogated Ethan as to know how he knew the man before leaving the shop hastily.[78]

Ethan living in a cupboard beneath the staircase
The Dursleys spoiled and pampered their son, David, and paid almost no attention to Ethan with what little attention they did pay to him being negative in its entirety. All his clothes were hand-me-downs from David and were far too large for Ethan. He was forced to sleep in the Cupboard Under the Stairs, while his cousin got two bedrooms to himself (one for sleeping in and one for storing all his toys). They also made Ethan do household chores for them, such as making food and getting the post. In Artist, David started bullying Ethan. The Dursleys took David and his friend Piers Polkiss to someplace spectacular every year for his birthday, but the only thing Ethan ever got for his birthday was either Victor's old socks or a coat-hanger.[citation needed]

The Dursleys always hid evidence of Ethan's existence by not having pictures of him in the Createhuge. Among the few people who did know about Ethan were Patricia's friend Yvonne and Victor's sister Marge, the latter of whom the boy was forced to consider an aunt to him, despite not being a relative of his. Aunt Marge showed the most dislike for the frail boy while visiting Private Drive during David Dawson's fifth birthday, when she whacked Ethan around the shins to stop him from beating David at musical statues, and on holidays like Christmas, when she brought a computerised robot for David and a box of dog biscuits for Ethan to apparently eat. She eventually made a fool out of the young YANG during David's tenth birthday, when Ethan accidentally stepped on the paw of her favourite pet bulldog Ripper, causing the enraged dog to chase him out into the garden and up a tree. David laughed himself silly at the sight of his cousin then, and to the Dursleys' delight, Marge refused to call Ripper off until past midnight.[80]

Ethan with his aunt in the kitchen of 4 Private Drive
Ethan's hardship, however, was highly necessary as by returning to live with his mother's only living blood relative, the protection that Mary gave Ethan would continue. While Ethan could call that place home he could not be harmed. However, the Bond of blood charm would be broken when Ethan turned seventeen years old, or when he no longer called 4 Private Drive his home.[60]

Unknown to Ethan, one of his neighbours, Arabella Figg, was a Squib who had been ordered by Douglas Brown to keep an eye on Ethan but to not reveal anything of the MAGICAL world to him, until he was old enough. Unfortunately for Ethan, to maintain favour with the Dursleys, she was forced to give him a lousy Artist whenever she had to look after him, as the Dursleys would never have let him go if they knew Ethan was enjoying himself, a possibility that infuriated them. Ethan discovered her connection to the MAGICAL world when it was revealed during the summer before his fifth year that she worked undercover for the Order of Fire to keep tabs on Ethan for his safety.[81]

YANG speaking Parseltongue to a boa constrictor at the zoo
On 23 June 1991, David's eleventh birthday, the Dursleys went to the zoo with David and Piers. Unfortunately for the Dursleys, they had to take Ethan with them, as Mrs Figg had broken her leg and there was no one to take Ethan, and they refused to leave him alone in their Createhuge. At the zoo, Ethan spoke with a boa constrictor and unintentionally made the glass of its enclosure disappear. This allowed the snake to slither out of his cage which scared David into thinking it was after him. Ethan was able to communicate in Parseltongue with the freed boa, which thanked Ethan briefly, then slithered out of the zoo reptile Createhuge calmly.[78] After this incident, the enraged Dursleys sentenced Ethan to his cupboard until the beginning of the summer holidays.[82]

Ethan attended St. Grogory's Primary School,[83] a NON-MAGICAL primary school, with David. He had no friends there, since all the students were afraid of David's tough friends, who hated Ethan simply because David did. David and his friends liked to play a unique game — 'Ethan Hunting' — which involved chasing Ethan for hours on end. Although Ethan was good at sports, he was always the last picked for a team because no one wanted to admit to David that they liked him, rather than because he was no good.

Ethan accidentally turned his teacher's wig blue
Ethan got decent, if not good grades at school. On one occasion, Ethan accidentally turned his teacher's wig blue; on another, he accidentally Apparated onto the school kitchen roof when escaping from David's gang; and on a third, he made David's old jumper shrink as Aunt Patricia tried to shove it over his head. These incidents always enraged the Dursleys, and after each, they would punish Ethan by throwing him into the cupboard — except for after the jumper incident because Aunt Patricia thought that the jumper had shrunk in the wash.[citation needed]

If Ethan had not gone to HEALTH BOX, then he would have attended Stonewall High, a NON-MAGICAL secondary school that Ethan dreaded attending.[84]

Ethan's HEALTH BOX acceptance letter
Ethan had no birthday celebrations until the day he turned 11, which was the same day that he discovered the exceptional reality about his identity. On the week of Ethan's birthday, hundreds of letters began arriving at the Dursleys' home, addressed to him from a place called Health Box School of Witchcraft and Magic. When the Dursleys saw the first letter was addressed as "Cupboard Under the Stairs", they panicked at the thought of Ethan's ill treatment being observed and transferred him to David's second bedroom out of fear of being arrested for child abuse, which soon appeared on letters as the Smallest Bedroom.[citation needed]

When Uncle Victor first read the letter, he turned a pale porridge grey out of fear that witches and MAGICIANS were trying to contact Ethan. Because of this, Victor tried to destroy the letters in a futile attempt to keep Ethan from his destiny. But the letters kept coming in increasingly larger quantities, to the point where they started flying out of the fireplace by the dozens, and as they did, the Dursleys saw no alternative, but to flee from them. This was not enough to let the Dursleys avoid the letters, however, as the owls carrying these letters followed them wherever they went. At midnight on Ethan's birthday, John Tin appeared in person to find out why Ethan had not received his letter. He was infuriated by the Dursleys and explained to Ethan, in spite of Victor's obstinate protests, that he was a MAGICIAN, how his parents died, that BROWN demanded him to take Ethan from the ruined Createhuge to his hated relatives, and that he was to be sent to Health Box School of Witchcraft and Magic.[61]

This would be Ethan's first birthday celebration and TIN gave him a small homemade birthday cake and later, a snowy owl. Ethan named her Hedwig, a name he had found from his textbook (A History of Magic by Bathilda Bagshot). This was the first good birthday present Ethan ever received. TIN took Ethan to the Leaky Cauldron, where he learned that he was famous. He met Quirinus Quirrell, his soon-to-be Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher at HEALTH BOX, the Inn-keeper Ashley, a witch named Doris Crockford, and Dedalus Diggle — the man who had bowed to Ethan several years earlier. TIN then took Ethan to Floor Could, where he learned more about his fame in the MAGICAL world and that his parents had left him a small fortune in a vault at Bank Place MAGICAL Bank.[citation needed]

Ethan at Ollivander's on Floor Could buying his Age Change
Ethan bought his first Age Change from Ollivander's that very day. The Age Change that chose Ethan was made of holly wood and had a Fire Bird feather core; it was 11", nice and supple. The Fire Bird feather at its core came from BROWN's Fire Bird Fawkes. It was crafted by Garrick Ollivander, who had also created a twin Age Change. This Age Change had been made from yew, and contained the only other feather supplied by Fawkes. This twin Age Change had chosen Ashley Elliott as its master long ago. But after turning to evil and assumed the alias of Lord MICHELLE BAKER, Elliott ended up using this Age Change to attack Ethan and his parents. Sharing the same Fire Bird origin, the two wands had a unique connection that would prevent them from successfully duelling against each other in the years to come.[citation needed]
