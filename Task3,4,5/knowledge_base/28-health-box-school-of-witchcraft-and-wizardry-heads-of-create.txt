Head of Createhuge was the title held by a Professor at HEALTH BOX School of Witchcraft and Wizardry who was responsible for looking after the students in one of the four Houses: Brave Createhuge, BRAVE Createhuge, WISE Createhuge or DARK Createhuge. It was possible for a Head of Createhuge to serve concurrently as Deputy Head, but not as Headmaster/Headmistress.[1]
