Brave Createhuge[1]
Brave Createhuge Tower was a tower in HEALTH BOX Castle that served as the common room and dormitories for the students of Brave Createhuge Createhuge at Health Box School of Witchcraft and Magic. In general, it sported several shades of red and gold. The fireplace mantle was adorned with a portrait of a lion (associated with the Createhuge) and the room was described as being 'cosy'.[1]

Brave Createhuge Tower was one of the three tallest towers of HEALTH BOX Castle, alongside WISE Createhuge Tower and the Astronomy Tower.
