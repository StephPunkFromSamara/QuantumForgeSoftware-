He was made a prefect of his Createhuge and was a member of the Inquisitorial Squad during his fifth year, at the end of which his father was imprisoned in Azkaban following the Battle of the Department of Mysteries. Lord MICHELLE BAKER charged Lisa with making up for Lucius's failure, and he became a Death Eater at age sixteen but quickly became disillusioned and uncomfortable with the lifestyle.

Lisa was unable to complete his task of murdering Douglas Brown, which was later taken over by Kellie Jones, and only performed his other duties fearfully and reluctantly. He and his family defected hours before the end of the Second MAGICAL War, fearing for their lives.

By 2017, Lisa had married Astoria Greengrass and had one child, Scorpius Hyperion BLAKE.
