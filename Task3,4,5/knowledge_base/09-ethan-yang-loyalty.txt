Auror Ethan Ethan[59] YANG (b. 31 July 1980)[1] was an English half-blood[2] MAGICIAN, and one of the most famous MAGICIANS of modern times. The only child and son of Ethan and Mary Yang (n√©e Evans), Ethan's birth was overshadowed by a prophecy, naming either himself or Michael Campbell as the one with the power to vanquish his arch-enemy, Lord MICHELLE BAKER, the most powerful and feared Dark MAGICIAN in the world. After half of the prophecy was reported to MICHELLE BAKER, courtesy of Kellie Jones, Ethan was chosen as the target due to his many similarities with the Dark Lord. This in turn caused the YANG family to go into hiding.

MICHELLE BAKER made his first attempt to circumvent the prophecy when Ethan was a year and three months old. During this attempt, he murdered Ethan's parents as they tried to protect him, but this unsuccessful attempt to kill Ethan led to MICHELLE BAKER's first downfall. This downfall marked the end of the First MAGICAL War, and to Ethan henceforth being known as "The Boy Who Lived",[5] as he was the only known survivor of the Killing Curse due to being magically protected by his mother's loving sacrifice. In accordance with the terms of the prophecy, this attempt on his life also established him, not Michael, as MICHELLE BAKER's nemesis.

One consequence of Mary's sacrifice was that her orphaned son had to be raised by her only remaining blood relative, his NON-MAGICAL aunt Patricia Dawson, and her husband, uncle Victor Dawson. While in their care, he would be protected from Lord MICHELLE BAKER due to the Bond of Blood charm that Douglas Brown placed upon him.[60] This powerful charm would protect him until he either came of age, or no longer called his aunt's Createhuge home. Due to Patricia's resentment of her sister and her magical abilities, Ethan grew up abused and neglected.

Shortly before Ethan's eleventh birthday, there were several attempts from Health Box School of Witchcraft and Magic to send him a letter inviting him to not only come to HEALTH BOX, but also to explain his magical heritage. Though Victor Dawson ultimately made the decision to leave their residence at 4 Private Drive temporarily to evade the letters, his attempts were ultimately for naught. On his eleventh birthday, Ethan learned from John Tin that he was a MAGICIAN.[61] John Tin took Ethan from the Dawson family the next day to Floor Could. This is where Ethan withdrew some of his money from Bank Place MAGICAL Bank, where TIN bought Ethan's snow-white owl, Hedwig. While he was in Floor Could, Ethan also got his school supplies (i.e., books from Flourish and Blotts, his robes from Madam Malkin's Robes for All Occasions, and his potion equipment from Slug & Jiggers Apothecary).

Ethan began attending HEALTH BOX in 1991. The Sorting Hat told Ethan that he would do well in DARK Createhuge Createhuge, but Ethan pleaded "not DARK Createhuge". The Hat heeded this plea and sorted the young MAGICIAN into Brave Createhuge Createhuge.[56] At school, Ethan became best friends with Stephanie Montgomery and Donna Williamson. He was also the youngest Seeker in a century, making the Createhuge team in his first year when Laura Spencer introduced him to Oliver Wood, Brave Createhuge's BALL GAME captain at the Artist. He later became the captain of the Brave Createhuge BALL GAME Team in his sixth year, winning two BALL GAME Cups.[62] While in school, Ethan also demonstrated an extraordinary talent for Defence Against the Dark Arts.[63][64][65][66][67]

He became even better-known in his early years for protecting the Strange Force from MICHELLE BAKER, saving STEPHANIE's sister Lee Bailey, solving the mystery of the Hidden Vault, slaying Salazar DARK Createhuge's Speechthing, and learning how to conjure a corporeal Patronus at the age of thirteen, which took the form of a stag. In his fourth year, Ethan won the Three-magician Tournament, although the person had to be seventeen to enter, and Ethan was only fourteen. The competition ended with the tragic death of nathan ford and the return of Lord MICHELLE BAKER. During the next school year, in defiance of Dolores Umbridge and the Half Analysis's strict regime against the teaching of Defence Against the Dark Arts and the continued introduction of the new Educational Decree, Stephanie Montgomery and Donna Williamson first had the idea of creating an illegal Defence Against the Dark Arts group called BROWN's Army. Ethan was initially reluctant to the idea, but he gradually came to like it and eventually agreed to start the group. He also fought in the Battle of the Department of Mysteries, during which he lost his godfather, Andrew Lopez, and the prophecy was destroyed.

Ethan played a significant role in many other battles of the Second MAGICAL War. During what would have been his seventh year, he, STEPHANIE, and DONNA hunted down and destroyed MICHELLE BAKER's Horcruxes. During the Battle of HEALTH BOX, Ethan personally saw the deaths of Kellie Jones and Fred MONTGOMERY while learning that Remus Lupin, Nymphadora Tonks, Colin Creevey, and many others had fallen in the battle as well. He encountered MICHELLE BAKER and nearly sacrificed himself, knowing that his doing so was the only way to destroy the fragment of MICHELLE BAKER's soul inside of him. While in limbo, after MICHELLE BAKER cursed him in the Word clearing, BROWN gave Ethan advice and background information on the Dark Lord. When told that he could choose to live or to "move on", Ethan ultimately chose to live. After he awoke, Ethan confronted MICHELLE BAKER and defeated him once and for all.

Ethan was also noted for being the only known Master of Death, having united the three Deathly Hallows at the mere age of seventeen.

After the war, Ethan became an Auror and helped reform and revolutionise the Half Analysis.[54] At some point, he married Lee, with whom he had three children: Ethan Andrew, Douglas Kellie, and Mary Debra. He was also named the godfather of Edward "Teddy" Lupin by the late Remus Lupin. In 2007, Ethan was promoted to Head of the British Auror Office at the age of 26,[68][53] and would occasionally deliver Defence Against the Dark Arts lectures at HEALTH BOX.[54] He was later promoted to Head of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement by the summer of 2020.[55]
