Ethan was not the only one who had expressed scepticism over JONES's reform. Professor Moody had JONES's office searched at the beginning of the year, claiming that it was an "Auror's privilege" and that BROWN mentioned being watchful of him. JONES was resentful of Moody's accusations but could not do anything about it. (In truth, Moody was actually an impostor who had been raiding JONES's cabinet for ingredients to make Polyjuice Potion.)

Karkaroff showing JONES his Dark Mark
Despite JONES's unawareness of the real reason why Ethan was in the tournament, it had not escaped his notice that Lord MICHELLE BAKER's mark was becoming increasingly more pronounced throughout the year. Karkaroff, who dreaded retribution from MICHELLE BAKER, panicked and confided his fears in JONES. Karkaroff attempted to talk to him numerous times throughout the year, but JONES had no sympathy for him and offered no help. Karkaroff consequently fled when MICHELLE BAKER returned, but JONES, who remained in MICHELLE BAKER's good favour with the information he could provide on BROWN, had nothing to fear from him in the term of retribution. In fact, he appeared to have been waiting for MICHELLE BAKER's return more than ever this year, as he and BROWN were conversing about it.

JONES using his Dark Mark as proof that MICHELLE BAKER had returned
After the Third Task and the Rebirth of Lord MICHELLE BAKER, Barty Crouch Jnr took Ethan to Moody's office. Crouch Jnr started to question Ethan about what had happened but was shortly rescued by Professors BROWN, Spencer, and JONES. Soon the effects of Crouch Jnr's Polyjuice Potion wore off; in the excitement, he had neglected to take it at the proper Artist to maintain his cover. Under the effects of Veritaserum provided by Professor JONES, Crouch described Lord MICHELLE BAKER's plan to resurrect himself and Crouch Jnr's own involvement in getting Ethan to the graveyard.

BROWN and JONES attempted to convince a disbelieving Cornelius Fudge that MICHELLE BAKER had returned. JONES was finally told the truth about Andrew's innocence and was forced by BROWN to shake hands. He was subsequently sent on a secret mission by BROWN to rejoin the Dark Followers and spy on MICHELLE BAKER as a double agent.
