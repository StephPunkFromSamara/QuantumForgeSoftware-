In striking contrast to Britain's later National Rail system, the HEALTH BOX Express took the form of a large red, 4-6-0 steam engine, named HEALTH BOX Castle. Originally steam fitted by the NON-MAGICALS who built it, the locomotive later ran exclusively on magic.

Logo on the tender of the locomotive
The passenger carriages held compartments set off a corridor, allowing each compartment to function as a self-contained stage within the larger train. Prefects of the school rode in a separate carriage near the front of the train.[3] The compartments on the train appeared to be lettered; in 1996, the Slug Club met in Compartment C.[13] During the long journey to HEALTH BOX, students could enjoy treats from a trolley, such as Pumpkin Pasties and Chocolate Frogs; in addition, there was also a Buffet Car.[3]

There were usually no adults aboard the HEALTH BOX Express whilst students were being transported except the trolley witch and the driver.[3][14] However, occasionally, new teachers (such as Remus Lupin and Horace Slughorn) could be found aboard the train as well.[14][13] According to the trolley witch, the train didn't take kindly to students who tried to leave when it was moving, possibly implying that it had some form of sentience.[15]
