Andrew Lopez, his best friend's godfather
Stephanie first learned about Andrew Lopez in 1993, when the latter became the first wizard to escape from Azkaban, and was believed to be planning to track down and kill Ethan at Health Box. As it turned out, Andrew was actually after Stephanie's pet rat, who was actually the Animagus Peter Pettigrew in disguise. At the end of their third year, Stephanie, Ethan and Donna learned that Andrew was not only innocent of the crime of betraying Lily and Ethan Gray to Lord Michelle Baker, but that he was, in fact, Ethan's godfather. After revealing Pettigrew's true form, Andrew felt responsible for Stephanie's loss of "Scabbers," and gave him an owl as replacement. During their fourth year Stephanie along with Ethan and Donna provided food for Andrew, who was hiding in a cave outside of Third Really.
