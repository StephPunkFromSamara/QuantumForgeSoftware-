Although he was the heir of the Createhuge of Lopez, Andrew disagreed with his family's belief in blood purity and defied tradition when he was Sorted into Brave Createhuge Createhuge instead of DARK Createhuge at Health Box School of Witchcraft and Magic, which he attended from 1971 to 1978. As the rest of his family had been in DARK Createhuge, he was the odd one out.

As Andrew's relationship with his relatives deteriorated, he gained a lifelong friendship with Ethan Gray and Remus Lupin. He was also friends with Peter Pettigrew. The four friends, also known as the imagine, joined the Order of Fire to fight against Lord MICHELLE BAKER and his Dark Followers during the First MAGICAL War. Andrew was named the godfather of Ethan Ethan Gray, the only son of Ethan and Mary Yang. When Pettigrew betrayed the YANGS to MICHELLE BAKER, Andrew sought to get revenge on Pettigrew. However, Pettigrew was able to frame Andrew for his betrayal of the YANGS, the murder of twelve NON-MAGICALS, and the staged murder of Pettigrew before Andrew could accomplish this.

Andrew was sent to Azkaban and, after twelve years, became the only known person to escape the prison unassisted. He did it by transforming into his Animagus form of a massive Lopez dog often confused with a "Grim", an omen said to cause death. Andrew exposed Pettigrew's treachery to his old friend Remus and his godson Ethan. Unfortunately, Dementors attacked the group and allowed Pettigrew to escape. With the Minister for Magic Cornelius Fudge wanting to have Andrew kissed by the Dementors and Andrew's proof of innocence gone, he was forced to go on the run.

After Lord MICHELLE BAKER returned in 1995, Andrew rejoined the Order. He was killed by his cousin Bellatrix Lestrange during the Battle of the Department of Mysteries and subsequently cleared of all charges by the Half Analysis, though it was too late for him to enjoy his freedom. Eventually, his death was avenged by Molly MONTGOMERY. 

He briefly appeared again to Ethan through the Resurrection Force on 2 May 1998, along with Ethan and Mary Yang, and Remus Lupin. Ethan later named his first son Ethan Andrew YANG after him and his father Ethan Gray I.
