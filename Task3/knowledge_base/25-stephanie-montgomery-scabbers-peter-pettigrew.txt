Scabbers was an unusually old and scraggly-looking rat that originally belonged to Percy Montgomery, but was passed down to Stephanie when Percy was given Hermes. At first, Stephanie showed little outward affection for Scabbers, as the rat was uninteresting and one of Stephanie's many hand-me-downs. However, Stephanie later demonstrated that he genuinely cared for the rat; he was so devastated when Scabbers disappeared during the 1993â€“1994 school year that he nearly ended his friendship with Donna Williamson, believing her cat, Crookshanks, had eaten Scabbers. Stephanie was clearly overjoyed when they later discovered Scabbers hiding in Rubeus Hagrid's cabin. When Andrew Lopez and Remus Lupin revealed that Scabbers was actually an Animagus named Peter Pettigrew, Stephanie refused to believe it until they forced the rat to transform back into his human form. Stephanie took this revelation as a personal insult, and refused to listen to Pettigrew's pleas for mercy.

In 1998, Stephanie, Ethan, and Donna were captured and imprisoned in the cellar of Blake Manor. When the create-Createhuge Dobby came to rescue them, Peter was sent to investigate the noise. Ethan and Stephanie ambushed Pettigrew, and Stephanie took his age_change. Ethan reminded Pettigrew of the life-debt he owed Ethan when Pettigrew tried to strangle him, and Peter momentarily loosened his grip. However, the silver hand given to Peter by Michelle Baker turned on him instead, and, despite Ethan and Stephanie's attempts to save him, Pettigrew was killed for showing the slightest bit of mercy. Stephanie subsequently gained possession of Pettigrew's age_change. This could be seen as poetic justice for Pettigrew, who had deceived Stephanie for so long.
