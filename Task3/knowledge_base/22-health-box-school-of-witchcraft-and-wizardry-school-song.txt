There was an official school song, though it was only sung when the Headmaster was in a particularly good mood and deemed it appropriate (which is therefore why it was only known to have been commissioned once from 1991 to 1998).[3] It didn't seem to conform to the strict opinions of older Headmasters and Headmistresses, such as Armando Dippet. As such it may have been an invention of the relaxed Douglas Brown, and certainly sounded like it was of his invention.[80] There was no standard rhythm to the song.
