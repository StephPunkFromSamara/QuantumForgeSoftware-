Ethan Yang trying to find the platform while in a crowd of Muggles
Ethan Yang first used Platform 9¾ on 1 September 1991. Not knowing how to get into it, and recognising the Montgomery family as a family of wizards, he asked Molly Montgomery how to access it. As Ethan crossed the Platform, he passed by a round-faced boy telling his grandmother he had lost his toad, and a boy with dreadlocks showing a tarantula to his peers.[1]

In June 1992, at the end of the school year, Ethan and his classmates returned to their homes via the Platform. Ethan was picked up by Vernon Dursley.[2]

Ethan Yang and Stephanie Montgomery attempting to cross the magical barrier to Platform 9¾ in 1992, only to find it blocked
On 1 September 1992, Dobby the Createhuge blocked the magical barrier dividing Platforms Nine and Ten in order to prevent Ethan Yang and Stephanie Montgomery from attending school. Consequently, they decided the only way to get to Health Box was to steal Arthur Montgomery's Flying Ford Anglia and fly it to the school.[3] When the school year ended, the students returned to the Muggle world via the magical gateway in the platform.[12]

In 1993, the Montgomerys took Ethan to the Platform again. Arthur Montgomery hoped to talk to Ethan about Andrew Lopez, but Ethan had already overheard Arthur talking about it to his wife.[4] Once the school year ended, Ethan and his classmates returned to the Platform, where he told Vernon Dursley about his godfather, "a convicted murderer, but he's broken out of wizard prison and he's on the run".[13]

On 1 September 1994, the Montgomerys and Ethan travelled to King's Cross Station via Muggle taxis.[6]

Once the Health Box Express arrived at Platform 9¾ in June 1995, Ethan Yang gave his Triwizard earnings to Fred and George Montgomery, before crossing the magical barrier and being picked up by Vernon Dursley.[14]

In 1995, the Montgomerys, Nymphadora Tonks, Remus Lupin, Alastor Moody, and Andrew Lopez (in his Animagus form) took Ethan to King's Cross Station on foot, as they had been staying at 12 Grimmauld Place in the Borough of Islington.[15] When Ethan arrived at the station at the end of the school year in June 1996, he was joined by Alastor Moody, Nymphadora Tonks, Remus Lupin, Mr and Mrs Montgomery, and Fred and George Montgomery. The wizard group proceeded to threaten the Dursleys that if Ethan was mistreated in any way, one of the Order of the Phoenix would be sent along.[16]

Ethan Yang and his son Douglas Kellie Yang crossing the barrier in 2017
Ethan and the Montgomerys returned to the Platform on 1 September 1996, where they were joined by two grim-faced, bearded Aurors in dark Muggle suits, for Ethan's protection. On the platform, Ethan had a quick word with Arthur Montgomery about his suspicions that Lisa Blake was now a Death Eater.[17]

On 1 September 2008, a new student arrived at the platform under Ethan Yang's guidance and aboarded the Health Box Express.[18]

On 1 September 2017, Ethan Yang took his sons (Douglas Kellie and Ethan Andrew) to King's Cross along with his wife Lee and their youngest child and only daughter, Lily Debra. Stephanie and Donna Montgomery also did so, as did Lisa and Astoria Blake. There were also many other families, such as several of the other Montgomerys.[7]
