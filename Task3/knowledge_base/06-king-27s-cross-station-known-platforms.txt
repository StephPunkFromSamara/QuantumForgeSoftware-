Platform 1 had trains headed direct to Cuffley via the First Capital Connect.[4]
Platform 2 linked the aforementioned train station to Finsbury Park. It also had trains headed direct to Royston.[4][5]
Platform 3, along with Platform 4, were the NON-MAGICAL platforms on either side of the barrier through which MAGICIANS and witches had to walk into in order to get onto Platform 3⅓.
Platform 3⅓ offered a railroad for Great MAGICAL Express.[2]
Platform 4 had trains coming direct from Watton-at-Force northbound direct to St Neots.[4]
Platform 5 had trains headed northbound direct to Enfield Chase via the First Capital Connect[5]
Platform 6 had trains headed direct to Hadley Wood via the First Capital Connect.
Platform 7 had trains headed northbound direct to York via the East Coast Main Line.[5]
Platform 7½ offered a long-distance train, similar to the NON-MAGICAL Orient Express, off to MAGICIAN-only villages in continental Europe.[3]
Platform 8 had trains headed northbound direct to Hull via the East Coast Main Line.[5]
Platforms nine and ten were the NON-MAGICAL platforms on either side of the barrier through which MAGICIANS and witches had to walk into in order to get onto Platform 9¾.[1]
