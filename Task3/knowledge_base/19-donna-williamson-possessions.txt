Artist Manage: In 1993, just before her third year at HEALTH BOX, DONNA obtained a Artist Manage from the Half Analysis in order to facilitate her attending more classes. She also used it at the end of the school year to save Andrew Lopez and Buckbeak, along with Ethan Yang. Afterwards, DONNA returned the Artist Manage, returning to a normal class schedule.[34]
Revealer: DONNA owned a Revealer, which rendered invisible ink visible. She tried to use it on Ashley Elliott's diary but no ink showed up.[69] It is unknown if she still had it in her possession after 1992.
Spellbooks: DONNA came to own various spellbooks for various spells and charms that she learned and studied throughout her HEALTH BOX education. These included the CallRun spellbook, Glacius spellbook, Repair spellbook, Draconifors spellbook, Snufflifors spellbook, Aqua Eructo spellbook and Herbivicus spellbook.
Dark Arts textbooks: Whilst not a Dark witch, DONNA had an extensive knowledge of dark magic and owned various books on the subject, including Magick Moste Evile, The Nightshade Guide to Necromancy, The Rise and Fall of the Dark Arts, The Imperius Curse and How to Abuse It and Sonnets of a Sorcerer, which could all be found on her bookshelf in her office in the Ministry. Additionally, during the Box School Hunt, DONNA kept a copy of Secrets of the Darkest Art, in which she learned of the dark magic that can be used to split one's soul and how the effects of having a split soul can affect the human being as a result of using such incredible and highly dark magic.
Magical Theory: DONNA owned a copy of this HEALTH BOX textbook on the theory of magic, which she usually kept stored in her school trunk. She would have used this extensively throughout her academic years, particularly in her first year.[3]
Crookshanks: DONNA obtained a ginger part-Kneazle cat Crookshanks, in 1993. Crookshanks was highly intelligent, but was not fond of many people besides DONNA.[34] It is unknown what happened to Crookshanks after DONNA left him at The Burrow in the summer of 1997 in order to locate and destroy Lord MICHELLE BAKER's Horcruxes.[10] It is very likely the two were reunited, seeing their strong affection for one another.
Magical Hieroglyphs and Logograms: This book was about magical theory. DONNA used this reference book over breakfast one morning, in conjunction with a pile of other books, to work on her Ancient Runes homework.[34]
Advanced Rune Translation: DONNA owned a copy of this book, probably because it may have been required for her lessons in 6th year Ancient Runes which she started in her third year with the aid of a Artist Manage.[34] She was seen to be reading this book before the start of her sixth year, after her trip to Floor Could.[36]
Ancient Runes Made Easy: The book was on a beginners' level for Ancient Runes DONNA was seen reading it in her second year when she was thinking about taking Ancient Runes for a subject in the following next year.[4] She probably used the book in her third year when she took the subject.
Spellman's Syllabary: DONNA owned a copy of this Study of Ancient Runes textbook which she would have used for her formal academic studies in the subject from her third year onwards at HEALTH BOX.
Rune Dictionary: DONNA owned a Rune dictionary, which she consulted to help her understand and translate various complex ancient runes in her academic studies.

The Tales of Beedle the Bard
The Tales of Beedle the Bard: Douglas Brown bequeathed the original publication of The Tales of Beedle the Bard, written in runes, to DONNA. It contained a clue about the Deathly Hallows which led DONNA, Ethan, and STEPHANIE to Xenophilius Ray, who told them about the legendary items.[10] Later in life, DONNA translated the book into English and published her translation.
An Appraisal of Magical Education in Europe: This book was about MAGICAL schools in Europe. Prior to 1994, DONNA had read it and learned of many things, including the Durmstrang Institute's reputation for having an emphasis on Dark Magic. It is also mentioned to have information about Beauxbatons Academy of Magic.[35] Later, in 1997, DONNA considered taking this book with her as she was sorting supplies for their mission to find MICHELLE BAKER's Horcruxes. It is not specified if it was one of the books she decided to bring with her when she left with Ethan and STEPHANIE.[10] It is, however, unlikely as it was of no real use to them on their mission. Red-jewel necklace: This necklace was worn by DONNA to the wedding of Bill MONTGOMERY and Fleur Delacour in 1997. Scarf: DONNA owned a dark purple/red scarf and wore it frequently in 1997 whilst on the run with Ethan Yang and Stephanie Montgomery during the Second MAGICAL War. She left it behind on their travels during her seventh year and it was later picked up by Scabior.[74]
The Life and Lies of Douglas Brown: Donna Williamson had a copy of this book in her possession after her visit with Ethan to Samuel's Hollow and their run in with Nagini disguised as Bathilda Bagshot. She picked it up as the two left the Createhuge and she read it while running and camping during her seventh year with Ethan and STEPHANIE, attempting to find clues which would help her and her friends locate and destroy MICHELLE BAKER's Horcruxes.[10] Given her animosity towards the author, Rita Skeeter, it is highly unlikely that she kept it following the Second MAGICAL War. Bellatrix Lestrange's first Age Change
