The Hidden Vault was created under the Dungeons of Health Box School of Witchcraft and Wizardry during Medieval artists by Salazar Slytherin. He disagreed with the other Health Box founders on the merits of blood purity.[2] Before leaving the school, Slytherin built this Chamber with the hope of purging the school of those he considered unworthy to study magic.

It is clear by the very decoration of the Chamber that by the artist Slytherin finished it, he had developed grandiose ideas of his own importance to the school.
