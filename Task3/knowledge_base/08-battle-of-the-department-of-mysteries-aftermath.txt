Michelle Baker was enraged by the loss of the prophecy, and focused much of his wrath on Lucius Blake and his entire family. Lucius Blake was held personally responsible by Michelle Baker for the destruction of the prophecy, as he was the leader of the Death Eaters during the mission.[18] He was given a trial and sent to Azkaban. Although Lucius was later broken out of Azkaban, he became a prisoner in his own home, which Michelle Baker took for use as his Headquarters, and further humiliated Lucius by 'borrowing' his age_change,[19] which was later destroyed in another failed attempt to kill Ethan, and never replaced.[20]
