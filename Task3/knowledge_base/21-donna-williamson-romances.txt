Viktor Krum, her friend and Yule Ball date
Viktor Krum, a renowned professional Quidditch player and student of Durmstrang, came to Health Box in 1994 for the Triwizard Tournament, in which he was a Champion. He took to following Donna into the library, watching her and trying to 'pluck up the courage' to speak to her. Eventually, he asked her to be his date to the Yule Ball, and Donna accepted. Their arrival together caused something of a stir, given Viktor's fame and Donna's exceptional, and rather unexpected, beauty.

Donna was Viktor's 'hostage' during the Second Task of the Tournament. After he pulled her from the lake, Viktor told Donna that he had never felt about someone the way he felt about her, and invited her to visit him in Bulgaria over the summer.[35] At some point, it is alleged by Lee Bailey during a confstephanietation with her brother Stephanie, that he and Donna shared a kiss during that period.[36] Whether or not Lee was just trying to get under Stephanie's skin or if this actually happened is unknown.
