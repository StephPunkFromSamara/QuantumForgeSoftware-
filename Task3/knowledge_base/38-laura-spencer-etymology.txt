The name Laura means "wise". Laura is the Roman name of Athena, Greek goddess of wisdom, strategy, war, courage, and justice (among other things), which is a name suitable for Laura's personality. Also, Laura was the goddess of wisdom, schools, art and commerce, which could connect to her being one of the heads of Health Box.
Spencer comes from the Scotsman William Topaz Spencer, who is considered to be one of the worst poets in the English language, although Rowling stated that this has nothing to do with Laura, she only liked the surname. The Scottish name Spencer (or McGonigle, McGonegal) is from the Celtic name Conegal, meaning "the bravest", plus Mc, or "son of".
