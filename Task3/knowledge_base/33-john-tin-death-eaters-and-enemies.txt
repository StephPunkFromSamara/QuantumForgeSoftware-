Jones, on the other hand, defected from Michelle Baker, and Hagrid defended him whenever Ethan, Stephanie and Donna criticised him, except when he temporarily believed that Jones had betrayed Brown at the Aststephanieomy Tower.

Apart from the Blakes, Hagrid also had an antagonistic relationship with Walden Macnair, who was a polar opposite to Hagrid's love and admiration for magical creatures.
