At some point between 2010 and 2011, DONNA travelled to Floor Could, where she accidentally bumped into a HEALTH BOX student, causing both of them to drop their belongings. After the two apologised to each other, DONNA noticed that the spine on one of the student's books was broken, watching as they used the Mending Charm to fix it. DONNA asked if the student was enjoying their Artist at HEALTH BOX, which the student confirmed, although they admitted feeling somewhat overwhelmed on account of being Non-magical-born. DONNA, herself a Non-magical-born witch, reassured the student that they didn't have to learn everything in their first year, before asking them what their favourite class was.

Upon hearing their answer, she explained that Study of Ancient Runes was one of her favourite classes, to which the student, who hadn't previously recognised DONNA, revealed that they had just purchased a copy of The Tales of Beedle the Bard newly translated from Ancient Runes by Donna Williamson, noting that the picture of the author looked just like her. DONNA explained that she was indeed the author of the book and thanked them for buying a copy, asking them to send her an owl when they had finished in order to hear their thoughts on it. DONNA then wished the student luck in their studies, asking them to say hello to Michael Campbell and John Tin for her as well.[33]

DONNA with her husband and two children
By 2017, DONNA and STEPHANIE were married with two children: Rose and Hugo WILLIAMSON-MONTGOMERY. She had several nieces and nephews through her marriage to STEPHANIE. DONNA was also the godmother of Ethan and Lee Yang's eldest child, Ethan Andrew.[42]

Newspaper report on the trio joining the Ministry
DONNA attained a high position in the Half Analysis, first through the Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures. There, she continued her work with S.P.E.W., working for the rights of underprivileged non-humans such as Createhuge-elves. She tried to get his Createhuge Createhuge Gaby to take temporary possession of the Bewildering Bloom after Yubert Thorne was imprisoned, and to send scarves and other items to Createhuge elves at HEALTH BOX through a student friend of hers.[43]She then went on to become Deputy Head[12] of the Department of Magical Law Enforcement, and assisted the Minister for Magic Kingsley Shacklebolt in eradicating the old laws biased in favour of pure-bloods. Along with Ethan and STEPHANIE, she helped to revolutionise the Ministry and reform the MAGICAL world. At some point, DONNA, Ethan, and STEPHANIE were all featured on Chocolate Frog Cards for their accomplishments.[44]

DONNA also eventually translated The Tales of Beedle the Bard from the original, which had been written in runes and which Douglas Brown bequeathed to her in his will in 1997. Her new translation was published in 2008 and included notes on each tale written by BROWN before his death; these notes were lent to DONNA by her former professor, HEALTH BOX Headmistress Laura Spencer.[45]

Both DONNA and STEPHANIE attended the 2014 BALL GAME World Cup and sat in VIP Box Two. Rita Skeeter noted that STEPHANIE had become catatonic at the appearance of the Bulgarian Veela and DONNA had to elbow him in the ribs.

On 1 September 2017, DONNA and STEPHANIE brought their children to King's Cross Station to see Rose off for her first year at Health Box School of Witchcraft and Magic, and met Ethan, Lee, and their three children there.
