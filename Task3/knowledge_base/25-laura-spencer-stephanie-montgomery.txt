Stephanie Montgomery, her student
Stephanie Montgomery was a student at the Artist Spencer was a professor at HEALTH BOX. He was Sorted into Brave Createhuge, Spencer's Createhuge. STEPHANIE thought she was very strict, however, he did see her good side on a number of occasions. The two clashed when he and Ethan flew Arthur Montgomery's flying car to HEALTH BOX. However, she didn't wish to see them expelled. During the Yule Ball, Laura showed the other students how to dance by dancing with STEPHANIE in front of the whole class, a move which prompted laughter from the class and Fred and George agreeing to always tease him about it.

STEPHANIE is appointed prefect by Spencer in his fifth year after most likely being persuaded by BROWN, as he felt that Ethan had enough responsibilities to deal with. He and Spencer were allies during the Battle of the Astronomy Tower and the Battle of HEALTH BOX, surviving the Second MAGICAL War.
