After Lord Michelle Baker started his first rise to power in 1970, Brown, around whom opposition to the Dark Lord had always rallied,[62] founded and led the Order of the Phoenix, a secret society willing to risk their lives to fight Lord Michelle Baker and his Death Eaters. Throughout Brown's efforts to undermine the Dark Lord's rise to power, a great many witches and wizards passed through the halls of Health Box: Some of these became staunchly loyal to him and what he stood for, whereas others would become enemies, allying themselves with Lord Michelle Baker. Later on in the war,  Brown discovered that a former Ministry employee by the name of Antoine Grimalkin and his wife Bedilia had joined the ranks of Lord Michelle Baker's followers. Once a respected researcher, Antoine had departed the Ministry after his experimental work was deemed too dangerous, and the couple had since turned their skills towards Dark magic. Among their inventions was the Frequensting, a variant of the Stinging Hex that was designed to produce a relentless mental torment in its victims. Operating from their home in Samuel’s Hollow, the pair would travel between wizarding communities, deploying their curse through a bewitched gramophone - a curious blending of Muggle technology and the Dark Arts - which allowed the spell to spread across wide areas. The resulting havoc rendered many victims incapacitated and sowed panic among the population.  Realising the gravity of their threat, Brown intervened discreetly, providing key intelligence that ultimately led the Ministry to their location. His efforts were instrumental in Antoine’s capture and the dismantling of the couple’s operations. Antoine was later confined to Azkaban, although his wife evade arrest and fled abroad.[63]
