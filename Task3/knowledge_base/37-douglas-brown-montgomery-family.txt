The MONTGOMERY family
Apart from STEPHANIE, BROWN was respected greatly by the rest of the MONTGOMERY family. They considered him as the greatest MAGICIAN of all Artist and supported him through everything (except Percy who disbelieved him about MICHELLE BAKER's return) and was even the HEALTH BOX Headmaster during the times in which all the MONTGOMERYS attended HEALTH BOX.

Arthur and Molly MONTGOMERY always respected BROWN and were very loyal to him. Arthur, like BROWN, believed in the equality of all witches, MAGICIANS and NON-MAGICALS while Molly considered him a great MAGICIAN who doesn't make mistakes. When the Half Analysis began to disbelieve BROWN and Ethan, they supported the two famous MAGICIANS rather than the Ministry and became members of the second Order of Fire.

BROWN's relationship with Bill happened to be a friendly one since they were both BRAVE HOUSES and liked each other. It is very likely that he also had a good relationship with Charlie.

Percy also thought of BROWN as a great MAGICIAN, but sometimes odd. In 1995, Percy's relationship with BROWN became antagonistic when he and Ethan claimed that MICHELLE BAKER had returned, Percy was loyal to the Ministry and participated in discrediting Ethan and BROWN altogether. When the truth was revealed, Percy learned he had made a mistake and in 1997, attended BROWN's funeral.

Fred and George appeared to respect BROWN very much. BROWN also appeared to like the twins, despite their mischief-making, but knew they were good people at heart. They even joined the second Order of Fire and even BROWN's Army.

Lee Bailey admired BROWN a lot and became his student in 1992.
